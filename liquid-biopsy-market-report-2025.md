## Liquid Biopsy industry Overview: Size and Growth Projections (Global & Regional Insights):

The liquid biopsy market focuses on non-invasive diagnostic techniques used to detect cancer and other diseases by analyzing blood or other body fluids. These tests detect circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA), circulating tumor cells (CTCs), RNA, and exosomes. Liquid biopsies are transforming oncology by offering real-time tumor profiling, early cancer detection, treatment monitoring, and relapse prediction. Their minimal invasiveness and ability to guide precision medicine make them a fast-growing segment in molecular diagnostics.

## Evolving Landscape of AI in the Liquid Biopsy in Healthcare Industry

AI is driving innovation in liquid biopsy by enhancing the interpretation of complex genomic data and improving diagnostic accuracy. Machine learning models help differentiate between benign and malignant signatures in ctDNA. AI tools are also used to process high-throughput sequencing data, detect rare mutations, and correlate biomarker profiles with clinical outcomes. Integration of AI enables faster, scalable, and more cost-effective cancer detection and monitoring.
