## Keratoconus Treatment industry Overview: Size and Growth Projections (Global & Regional Insights):

The keratoconus treatment market focuses on managing a progressive eye disorder where the cornea thins and bulges into a cone-like shape, causing vision distortion. Treatment options include eyeglasses, rigid gas-permeable contact lenses, corneal cross-linking (CXL), intrastromal ring segments (INTACS), and in severe cases, corneal transplantation. Rising awareness, improved diagnostic tools, and technological advances in minimally invasive procedures are driving the market. The demand is growing, especially among adolescents and young adults.
