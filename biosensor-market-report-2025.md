## Biosensor industry Overview: Size and Growth Projections (Global & Regional Insights):

The biosensor market comprises analytical devices that detect biological molecules and convert them into measurable signals. Used across healthcare, agriculture, food safety, and environmental monitoring, biosensors support real-time and point-of-care diagnostics. In healthcare, they are widely used for glucose monitoring, infectious disease testing, cardiac biomarker detection, and wearable health tracking. Increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, demand for rapid diagnostics, and rising use of smart devices are driving the market forward.
