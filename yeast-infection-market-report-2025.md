## Yeast Infection industry Overview: Size and Growth Projections (Global & Regional Insights):

The yeast infection market focuses on treating fungal infections, particularly those caused by Candida albicans, which affect the skin, mouth, genitals, and bloodstream. These infections are common among immunocompromised individuals, pregnant women, and patients using antibiotics or corticosteroids. The market includes antifungal drugs (azoles, polyenes, echinocandins), over-the-counter topical treatments, and diagnostics. Growing awareness, rising incidence of vaginal yeast infections, and drug-resistant strains are driving market demand.
